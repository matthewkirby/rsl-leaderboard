Rules
  1. You must use the most recent version of the RSL script. (Seeds rolled by RSLBot are always on the most recent version.)
  2. You must ping @Random Settings in the OoTR Discord and have your race open to all participants.
  3. Follow the Universal Rules and the Standard ruleset, with a few exceptions listed below:
  4. Crossing the Gerudo Valley bridge as a child shall be banned unless it is from back to front.
  5. Crossing the Gerudo Valley bridge as an adult shall be banned unless the bridge itself is repaired, or you have Epona or Longshot.
  6. Child in Gerudo Fortress is unbanned in all circumstances.
  7. Timing will end differently based on the gamemode:
    1. If it is a “Beat Ganon” seed, standard timing rules apply; .done is on the first frame of the cutscene that plays after beating Ganon.
    2. If it is a “Triforce Hunt” seed, .done will be on the first frame the game fades completely to black after obtaining the last required piece.
