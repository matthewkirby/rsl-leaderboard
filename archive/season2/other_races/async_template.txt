Rated Async <##>
<YYYY>-<MM>-<DD>T00:00:00Z
<url to racetime user profile>,<race placement>,<racetime username>,<race status. Either done or dnf>,P0DT<HH>H<MM>M<SS>S

Repeat the bottom line for each entrant to the race. They do not need to be in order.
